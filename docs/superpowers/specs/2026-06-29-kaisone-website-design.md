# Kaisone Website Launch Design

## Objective

Launch a credible, conversion-oriented website for Kaisone as a broad custom-software company serving businesses, organizations, schools, and founders.

## Approved Positioning

**Headline:** Custom software built around how your business actually works.

**Subheading:** Kaisone builds practical internal tools, automation systems, AI-powered workflows, and prototypes that replace inefficient manual processes.

**Primary action:** Discuss your project.

## Approved Offers

1. Workflow Fix - starting from TZS 900K
2. Prototype Sprint - starting from TZS 1.2M
3. Operations System - starting from TZS 3M
4. System Care - starting from TZS 350K/month

The private 5-7 day Workflow Pilot is reserved for qualified sales conversations and is not public website copy.

## Information Architecture

1. Header: brand, Services, Selected work, Process, About, and primary CTA.
2. Hero: approved positioning over a general business-operations image.
3. Services: four expandable approved offers with starting prices.
4. Selected work: clearly labelled internal products and prototypes, not client engagements.
5. Process: Understand, Define, Build, Handover.
6. About: Kaisone's practical, workflow-first approach.
7. Contact: structured project inquiry, optional WhatsApp CTA, and truthful delivery state.
8. Privacy: standalone explanation of collection, purpose, storage, access, processors, retention, and deletion requests.

## Visual System

Direction: **Editorial Operations**. Use restrained dark working surfaces, paper-white process sections, documentary photography, the exact supplied monogram, and controlled motion.

- Ink 950: `#080A0D`
- Ink 900: `#101419`
- Graphite: `#1A2026`
- Divider: `#39434B`
- Paper: `#F2F1EC`
- Fog: `#C7CDD1`
- Muted: `#929BA2`
- Signal Mint: `#36D6A5`
- Error Coral: `#FF6B5E`, errors only
- Typography: Instrument Sans and IBM Plex Mono
- Geometry: fine dividers, open sections, no decorative card grid
- Motion: restrained and disabled under `prefers-reduced-motion`

## Media Rules

The hero may depict a general East African business workflow but must not imply a real Kaisone client. Portfolio visuals must use approved internal screenshots or fictional demonstration data. Personal records, member identities, financial information, credentials, and private communications must be removed.

## Contact Rules

- Save a local draft before attempting delivery.
- Show success only after a configured endpoint returns success.
- Without an endpoint, copy the structured inquiry and disclose that direct delivery is not connected.
- Hide WhatsApp when no approved number is configured.
- Do not publish a personal address or placeholder contact destination.

## Non-Goals

No blog, CMS, accounts, dashboard, chatbot, online payments, fake case studies, testimonials, partner logos, performance metrics, or Workflow Pilot pricing in this launch.

## Verification

Automated tests cover positioning, offers, integrity, contact states, conditional WhatsApp, and privacy routing. Browser QA covers desktop/mobile layout, navigation, service expansion, form validation, overflow, focus behavior, reduced motion, images, and console errors.
