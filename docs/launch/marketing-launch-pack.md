# Kaisone Launch Marketing Pack

## Launch Announcement

Kaisone builds custom software around the way a business actually works. We create focused internal tools, automation systems, AI-powered workflows, and prototypes that replace inefficient manual processes. If an important workflow is stuck across paper, spreadsheets, or disconnected tools, discuss the process with us.

## Seven-Day Content Sequence

1. **Launch:** Introduce Kaisone, its approved positioning, and the project discussion CTA.
2. **Operational friction:** Show how repeated copying, missed follow-ups, and disconnected records create avoidable work.
3. **Internal-tool demonstration:** Present a sanitized Kaizen System workflow and label it as an internal system.
4. **Workflow Fix:** Explain what one focused automation can change and publish the TZS 900K starting price.
5. **How Kaisone works:** Explain Understand, Define, Build, Handover.
6. **Prototype Sprint:** Explain how a working prototype reduces uncertainty before a larger investment.
7. **Invitation:** Ask owners and operators to bring one inefficient process for a focused discussion.

Every post must use real product visuals or clearly labelled fictional demonstration data. Do not publish client counts, outcome percentages, testimonials, or partnerships without evidence and permission.
